BuildPulse transforms construction site monitoring by combining 3D model visualization with Google Gemini AI vision. Instead of spending 2+ hours on manual progress assessments, your team uploads a daily photo and receives an instant breakdown of what's complete, what's missing, and whether the project is on track to meet its deadline.

## How BuildPulse works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload your 3D model">
    Import a `.glb` or `.gltf` building model (up to 50MB). BuildPulse stores it in MongoDB GridFS and renders it interactively in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign trackable zones">
    Click directly on mesh surfaces in the 3D viewer to define zones — Kitchen, Roof, Foundation, Exterior Walls, and more. Each zone becomes an independent tracking unit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check in with a photo">
    On-site, snap a photo of the construction area and upload it to the zone. Gemini 2.0 Flash compares the photo against your 3D model render and returns a completion estimate with confidence rating.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor and forecast">
    Your dashboard aggregates zone progress, plots completion velocity over time, and warns you when the project is at risk of missing its target date — before it's too late to act.
  </Step>
</Steps>
